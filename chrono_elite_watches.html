Our Story

Founded on Passion, Perfected Through Time

Chronos Elite was born in 1962 from a single watchmaker's dream: to create timepieces that transcend utility and become objects of enduring beauty. Our atelier in Geneva remains the beating heart of every watch we create.

Today, Chronos Elite represents the pinnacle of horological engineering — a fusion of Swiss tradition and contemporary design that appeals to those who understand that true luxury lies in the details only the discerning eye can see.

Swiss Movement

Every mechanism is hand-assembled by certified master watchmakers in Geneva, Switzerland.

Lifetime Guarantee

Each timepiece comes with a comprehensive lifetime service warranty and dedicated concierge.

Ethically Sourced

All materials — from sapphire crystals to 18k gold — are certified conflict-free and sustainable.
